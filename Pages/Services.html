Our Services

Fireguard provides cutting-edge fire safety and emergency response services powered by smart technology. We're committed to protecting lives, properties, and businesses across Nigeria and beyond.

What We Offer

Real-Time Fire Alerts

Get instant notifications via mobile and web when a fire is detected in your vicinity. Our system uses smart triggers and sensor data to alert you early—giving you time to act and stay safe.

Smart Sensor Integration

Connect our system with IoT-enabled smoke, gas, and heat detectors. Automate fire detection in homes, offices, factories, and public spaces with minimal human intervention.

Interactive Fire Maps

Visualize fire-prone zones, active incidents, and emergency units on a live map. We use GPS and crowdsourced data to guide users away from danger and toward safety.

Emergency Coordination

Coordinate directly with the nearest fire station or emergency responders. Our system shares your live location and situation status to enable faster dispatch and action.

Offline Mode Access

Even without internet access, our platform keeps you informed. Access emergency guidelines, evacuation steps, and preloaded safety protocols anytime, anywhere.

Fire Incident Reporting

Witness a fire or hazard? Report it directly through our app with photos, location pins, and real-time notes. Help alert others and support quick response efforts.
