About Fireguard

Revolutionizing fire safety with technology-driven solutions.

Who We Are

Fireguard is a modern fire safety platform built to protect lives and property through cutting-edge technology. We combine real-time alerts, smart detection, and seamless emergency coordination into one easy-to-use solution.

Founded in Abuja, Nigeria, Fireguard was born out of a desire to improve emergency response time, empower individuals with safety tools, and support firefighting agencies with actionable data.

Our Mission

To harness the power of technology to reduce fire outbreaks and response time across Africa and beyond.

Our Vision

To be the leading fire safety tech solution trusted by individuals, institutions, and emergency services globally.
