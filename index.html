Features

Real-Time Fire Alerts

Receive immediate fire notifications the moment danger is detected. Fireguard uses advanced location tracking and hazard detection algorithms to alert users of fire outbreaks near them, ensuring you and your loved ones have enough time to evacuate or take preventive measures.

Smart Sensor Integration

Fireguard seamlessly integrates with modern IoT devices such as smoke detectors, temperature sensors, and fire alarms. When a connected sensor is triggered, Fireguard processes the data and instantly communicates with emergency services. This allows for faster response times and automates fire safety in homes, offices, or large buildings.


User Location Tracking

Fireguard enables you to track your real-time location and share it securely with loved ones, team members, or rescue teams during a fire emergency. This ensures that help can reach you quickly and that your whereabouts are always known during critical moments. You can even assign trusted emergency contacts to receive automatic location updates.

24/7 Monitoring & Support

Fireguard offers round-the-clock monitoring services backed by AI systems and human support. Our platform ensures that fire-related activity is always being tracked and that you can access help whenever you need it. Whether it's 2 AM or during a holiday, you’ll never be alone in a crisis.


Offline Safety Mode

We understand that not all emergencies happen with stable internet access. That’s why Fireguard is equipped with an offline mode that stores essential fire safety guides, preloaded maps, and quick emergency instructions. In moments of poor network reception, you’ll still have access to life-saving information and protocols to guide you through fire emergencies.

Incident Reporting

Be a community hero by reporting fire outbreaks quickly through our easy-to-use interface. Users can upload photos, record videos, tag locations, and share eyewitness accounts that are reviewed and forwarded to appropriate authorities. This crowdsourced system helps build a network of active safety observers, making fire prevention a shared responsibility.
